Life is a dance, choose how you want to move!

STANDARD BALLROOM DANCE

Experience the grace of fluid motion and impeccable posture. This discipline comprises five classic dances: Waltz, Tango, Viennese Waltz, Slow Foxtrot, and Quickstep. It is a masterclass in tradition, sophistication, and technical harmony.

LATIN DANCES

An explosion of energy, speed, and raw emotion. This category features the Samba, Cha-Cha-Cha, Rumba, Paso Doble, and Jive. These dances celebrate athletic power, rhythmic precision, and soulful expression.

CARRIBEAN DANCES

The heat of Salsa, the romance of Bachata, and the joyful pulse of Merengue. These styles focus on social connection and vibrant hip movement, bringing the sun-drenched spirit of the Caribbean to the dance floor.

FORMATION DANCES

When individuality transforms into unity. Teams of couples perform choreographed routines in either the Standard or Latin styles. Witness a breathtaking display of synchronization as dancers move as one, creating complex geometric patterns at lightning speed.
